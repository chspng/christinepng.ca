A brief background

I began by studying life sciences and quickly discovered a love of programming and design. Nowadays I'm working toward finishing my Master's degree in Biomedical Communications, where I'm honing my skills in design, data visualization, storytelling, programming, and illustration. When not freelancing or working on my Master's project, I can be found reading a good book, walking through the woods, or reflecting on how to build strong communities.
